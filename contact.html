About Us

Arngren Electronics is a well-established company specializing in a wide range of electronics, gadgets, and postal services. Founded in 1983 by Frithjof Arngren, the company initially focused on providing high-quality products to local customers. In 1994, Arngren Electronics revolutionized the industry by venturing into mail-order sales, becoming a pioneering force in the field. This innovative approach helped the company expand its reach and solidify its position in the market.

However, in the late 1990s, the company underwent a significant transition when it was sold to investor Simon Yuen and several associated foundations in 1999-2000. Under new ownership, Arngren Electronics shifted its operations to an online model, capitalizing on the emerging e-commerce landscape. Unfortunately, despite these efforts, the company struggled financially and was forced into bankruptcy within a year.

In a remarkable turn of events, Arngren Electronics was able to acquire the bankrupt estate and return to its roots, operating through the popular gaming platform, GameWorld. Today, the company has built an impressive customer base of over 130,000 names on its mailing list, reflecting its continued influence and legacy.

Despite its success, Arngren Electronics has faced significant criticism regarding the design of its websites. The company is actively working to improve user experience and address these concerns, with plans to update and modernize its online presence.
